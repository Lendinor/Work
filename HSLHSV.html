HSL/HSV

RGB and Introduction Hexadecimal HSL/HSV

This page is dedicated to our final section/topic/subject/thing. This subject is HSL/HSV. This means hue, saturation, and lightness(luminosity) or hue, saturation, value(brightness for hsb). There is a third variety HSI hue, saturation, intensity. These are also based on RGB. This leads me to believe RGB is the most important and all others are not worth learning. But the purpose of HSL/HSV was to be more intuitive than the RGB cube. It finds use in computer graphics, color pickers, and image editing.
